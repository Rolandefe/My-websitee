About Roland

Artist • Storyteller • World Builder

Who Is Roland?

Kalegha Roland, known professionally as Roland, is an artist who blends Trap, Afrobeats, R&B and Pop into a unique sound that connects emotion with storytelling.


Beyond music, Roland is also a writer, creating original fantasy worlds, cinematic stories and unforgettable characters, including The Last Flower of Avalarose.



Musical Style

• Trap
• Afrobeats
• R&B
• Pop



Creative Vision

Roland believes that music and storytelling should transport people into new worlds. Every song, every script and every project is created to inspire emotion, imagination and unforgettable experiences.



Current Projects

🎵 Creating new music and expanding his catalogue.

🌹 Developing The Last Flower of Avalarose, an original fantasy movie script filled with mystery, curses and adventure.

🌍 Building a creative universe where music, stories and visual art come together.
