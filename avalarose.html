The Last Flower of Avalarose

One flower. One curse. One final chance to save the world.

Synopsis

In a world once blessed with peace, the actions of one girl and a mysterious flower awaken an ancient curse long thought forgotten. As the curse spreads, people transform into insatiable beasts, plunging society into chaos.

With civilization on the brink of collapse, a group of high school teenagers must uncover the truth behind the curse, survive monstrous threats, and fight to restore balance before the world is consumed.

Main Character

Roland

A determined teenager thrust into a battle far greater than himself. Roland becomes one of the key figures in humanity's struggle against the curse.
