The Last Flower of Avalarose

A Fantasy Movie Script by Roland Kalegha

Story

In a world that once knew peace, the actions of one girl and a mysterious flower awaken an ancient curse. As the curse spreads, people are transformed into insatiable monsters, throwing civilization into chaos. The fate of the world rests in the hands of a group of high school teenagers who must uncover the truth, break the curse and restore balance before humanity is lost forever.

The Ancient Curse

Hidden for centuries, the Ancient Curse awakens once again after the mysterious Last Flower of Avalarose is disturbed. Those consumed by the curse gradually lose their humanity, becoming relentless monsters driven only by instinct and destruction. As fear spreads across the world, every passing day brings civilization closer to collapse.



The World of Avalarose

Avalarose is a world where ancient legends, forgotten kingdoms and supernatural forces coexist with modern society. Beneath everyday life lies a hidden history of powerful relics, mysterious creatures and secrets waiting to be uncovered.
