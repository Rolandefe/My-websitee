About The Artist

Kalegha Roland, known professionally as Roland, is an artist who blends Trap, R&B, Pop, and Afrobeats into a sound designed to inspire, uplift, and energize listeners. Through powerful melodies, emotional storytelling, and modern production, Roland is creating a unique musical identity and setting the stage for even greater projects ahead.
