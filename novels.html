The Last Flower of Avalarose

Fantasy • Romance • Adventure • Mystery

In a world once experiencing peace, the actions of one girl and a mysterious flower lead to the resurfacing of an ancient curse. As the curse spreads, humans transform into savage, insatiable creatures, threatening the very existence of civilization. With the world descending into chaos, a group of high school teenagers must uncover the truth behind the curse, confront terrifying monsters, and fight to restore peace before all hope is lost.

Protagonist: Roland
